AGENT

SAWI

Guardian

Sawi is a guardian of ancient healing arts, using mystical energy to protect allies and control space. Unlike traditional healers, she blends defense with tactical barriers, ensuring her team stays in the fight while keeping enemies at bay.

ABILITY

Life Bloom

Sawi channels healing energy in a small radius, gradually restoring health to allies within the zone. This ability affects multiple teammates but requires them to stay inside the area.
