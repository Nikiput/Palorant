AGENT

JEZZ

Striker

Jez is a lightning-fast warrior who dominates the battlefield with unmatched agility and precision. A master of aerial combat, she dashes through enemies and eliminates them before they can react. With her high-speed movement and deadly accuracy, Jez is built for players who thrive on outplaying opponents with pure skill.

ABILITY

vortex veil

Jez throws a fast-moving wind orb that expands into a swirling vortex on impact, blocking vision and slightly pulling enemies towards its center.
