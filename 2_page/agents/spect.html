AGENT

SPECT

Guardian

Spect is a master of information warfare, utilizing a network of advanced surveillance tools to keep enemies at bay. With his surveillance capabilities, he locks down areas, gathers intel, and denies the enemy any chance to slip past his watchful eyes.

ABILITY

Smoke Coil

Spect throws a device that, when activated, releases a cloud of dense smoke in a small radius. Enemies passing through the smoke are briefly slowed, and their vision is heavily obstructed.
