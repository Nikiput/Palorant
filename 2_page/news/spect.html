Meet SPECT: The Phantom Watcher

Published September 1, 2024 at 09:55 PM

Palorant_Admin1

A shadow moves where no one is looking—a presence felt, but never seen. Enter Spect, the newest agent to join the fight, bringing deception, surveillance, and a deadly mind to the battlefield. Armed with high-tech gadgets and a masterful understanding of information warfare, Spect is always one step ahead, ensuring enemies never act without consequence.

A SHADOWED PAST

Little is known about Spect’s origins, but whispers suggest he once belonged to an elite intelligence unit that specialized in counter-surveillance and psychological warfare. Now, he fights on his own terms, ensuring that no enemy moves unnoticed. A silent observer, a manipulator of vision—Spect is always watching.

ABILITIES OVERVIEW

Host Eye

Deploy a floating surveillance drone that automatically tracks enemy movement within its range. Once locked, enemies will be briefly revealed even after they leave its sight.

Phantom Wires

Lay down nearly invisible tripwires that detect, slow, and disrupt enemies who pass through them. The wires can be remotely detonated to cause a brief disorienting effect.

Hollow Veil

Throw a special smoke device that creates a one-way vision field, allowing Specter and allies to see through while keeping enemies blind to what’s on the other side.

Players won’t have to wait long to see Specter in action. A full gameplay showcase is on its way, offering a first look at how this tactical mastermind operates in live combat. Keep your eyes open—because Specter will be watching.
