GAME UPDATE

Palorant First Patch Note 01.01

Published November 10, 2024 at 07:55 AM

Nikita Put

The latest update is here! Patch 1.1 introduces a brand new Agent, a fresh map, and essential balance changes for the game. Check out the full patch notes below!

BUG FIXES & QOL UPDATES

  • Fixed issues with ability collision detection on certain maps.
  • Adjusted hit registration to improve accuracy across all ranges.
  • Updated crosshair customization with more options.

BALANCE CHANGES

Tactictor
  • Slight increase in smoke duration for more impactful area denial.
  • Phase Shift now affects a wider radius to help disrupt enemy plans.
Guardian
  • Shield Beacon now absorbs more damage, making it harder to destroy.
  • Reduced Healing Aura cooldown for quicker team support.
Striker
  • Increased Silent Shots range slightly for better positioning and execution.
  • Reaper's Fury now has a smaller cooldown to make it more reliable in intense fights.
